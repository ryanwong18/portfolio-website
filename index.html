About Me

Hey there, I'm Ryan!

Originally from Calgary, the Rocky Mountains were a landmark of my early life, but in 2015 I packed my bags and moved to Toronto. The CN Tower is now what I wake up to every morning - not too shabby!

Holding to my roots, I continue to enjoy hiking and camping when the opportunities arise, but I've also come to appreciate the diversity of experiences that Toronto has to offer. In my spare time, I also enjoy reading and playing a variety of sports.

Before becoming a Front-End Developer, I spent four years in the Oil & Gas industry as a Professional Engineer and spent almost three years in Finance at a large Canadian financial institution. With a desire to continue learning, I recently completed the full-time bootcamp at HackerYou.

Coding is a new puzzle that I'm continuing to solve and it challenges both my creativity and analytical ability. JavaScript and React are the reasons I get up in the morning, but they are also the reasons I took up coffee again!
